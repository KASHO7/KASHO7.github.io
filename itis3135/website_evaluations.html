Website Evaluation

Dribbble

Dribbble.com is a global online hub for designers to showcase their creative work, connect with peers, and find inspiration.

  • Design: Clear contrast between the vibrant and colorful design shots and the clean, white background of the website. This contrast ensures that the design work stands out and grabs the user's attention.
  • Repetition: Maintains a consistent grid layout for displaying design shots. Each design shot is presented in a rectangular container with uniform dimensions, creating a repetitive and organized structure.
  • Alignment: Navigation elements such as menus and buttons are horizontally and vertically aligned, ensuring a cohesive and user-friendly interface.
  • Proximity: Comments and discussions related to a specific design shot are positioned in proximity to that shot.

Berkshire Hathaway

Berkshire Hathaway's website is for investors and people curious about the company. It has details on the company, its money, resources for investors, and news about its many businesses.

  • Contrast: Websites that use a wide range of colors inconsistently throughout their pages can appear chaotic and unprofessional, causing confusion for users.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent placement of navigation menus, logos, or buttons can make it challenging for users to navigate a website efficiently
  • Alignment: Does not fully utilize contemporary alignment techniques to create visually compelling layouts.
  • Proximity: Websites that place too many elements in close proximity can overwhelm users and make it difficult for them to focus on important information.
